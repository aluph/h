fonsyf:_

A device that acts as a mobile phone holder for keeping your hands germ free each time you use your mobile

context

In 2020, a global pandemic of COVID-19 has turned the world upside down. The contagious nature of this virus and the quickness of spreading has created more awareness of transmission of germs among the general masses. This has also brought the realization of how objects in our daily life act as a medium for infection. Recent studies have shown that the COVID 19 virus can survive on surfaces like metal and plastic for up to 3 days. 

 

One object that is universally used is the mobile phone and due to its vast usage, mobile phone surfaces have become a breeding ground for viruses and bacteria. With the spreading rate of Covid19, it is advised by health professionals to keep your phone sanitized and make sure it is germ free.

problem 

In this Pandemic scenario the frequent usage of cell phones can also be a major mode of transmission for the virus from infected persons due to surface contact.

 

Despite washing hands multiple times a day or wearing gloves, cross contamination can happen if your mobile phone is not sanitary. Since mobiles are carried around and being touched extensively and being held to the face, unhygienic mobile phones cause a serious health risk. Various studies conducted around the world have shown the amount of microbes and germs present on a cellphone. The standard procedure for preventing the spread of the Covid 19 virus is to wash your hands and avoid touching the face. When you follow these guidelines, you often overlook the element that most of us use in close proximity to our face – the phone!

Over usage

How often does a person wash his/ her hands before using mobile phones? The amount of times a person uses their gadget per day without a secondary surface contact is uncountable. 

In a time of social distancing, we are all trying to connect through the internet and social media. From the offices to the kitchen, mobile phones are a priority for a comfortable life. People use it while cooking, driving, for reading, to catch up on their TV shows, to attend classes etc.Mobile phones have made our lives easier in terms of maintaining relationships and betterment of lifestyle. Recently a trending video by a nurse demonstrated how despite wearing gloves, cross contamination can ultimately get the virus on our body. Similarly no matter how many times you wash your hand, your dirty phone may reverse the effects of hand washing. Mobile phones can also pose a serious health risk if we do not take the necessary precautions

Sanitization

Cleaning mobile phones every time with anti-bacterial solutions all the time is also not a practical solution as it could affect the screen performance if it’s not done right. Not to mention the hassle you go through as the device is used extensively throughout the day

solution

The only practical solution to follow is to sanitize hands before phone usage and ensure that your phone is clean that reduces the carrying of germs.

  

Fonsyf is a novel solution to this problem to be more precautious by enabling a phone lock that unlocks only after cleaning hands with an inbuilt sanitizer to be completely germ free. It’s built with an idea that something is better than nothing. This classy and sleek product works on a simple push mechanism inspired from pistol recoil spring technique. It requires very primitive technology and knowledge to work on.

how does it funtion?

Fonsyf is designed with a pragmatic technological approach to make it easier to experience. The basic mechanism applied here is inspired from the pistol spring recoil technique making it easier to manufacture.

Step 1-

Fonsyf follows very basic steps to be used. Firstly the user’s mobile phone is inserted into the top pocket completely. Ideally the phone should be wiped clean with sanitizing wipes or alcohol wipes to make sure that there are no germs present while inserting.

Step 2-

To take the phone out the user is forced to clean their hands by applying sanitizer dropping from the topmost handle when pushed in the downward direction.

This simple mechanism keeps the user secured from germs transferring to the mobile phones from other surfaces in contact.

To take the phone out the user has to push the handle in the downward direction which ejects sanitizer onto the hand. As the handle is pushed down simultaneously the internal mechanism unlocks the bottom lock allowing the adjacent piston to move upwards.  The user can now sanitize their hands and use the phone with clean hands. When the Fonsyf runs out of sanitizer, it can be refilled making it an eco-friendly product!

With and without fonsyf

With fonsyf

Fonsyf works as a safe storage for your phone. Fonsyf ensures that your phone does not come in contact with other surfaces minimizing the surface contamination and making your hand washing 100% effective! Your clean hands can now hold a clean phone and reduce your stress about germs and diseases.

Without fonsyf

Washing your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds makes them clean, but your hands come into contact with many surfaces before you touch your phone transferring the germs onto your hand and eventually your phone. So the next time you wash your hands, your hands may be clean but your phone is still infected! This can be an endless spiral of contamination which causes nuisance and stress.
